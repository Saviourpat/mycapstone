About Task Manager

Task Manager is an intuitive tool designed by 3MTT Group 3 to help you stay organized, track your tasks, and increase productivity. Whether you're managing your personal tasks or coordinating with a team, Task Manager streamlines the process and makes task management simple and efficient. Our platform provides the flexibility to assign tasks, set deadlines, track progress, and collaborate with others in real-time.


Task Manager offers a range of features to ensure that all your task management needs are met. With a user-friendly interface, you can easily:


Create and manage tasks, including setting deadlines and reminders. Collaborate with team members, assign tasks, and track progress. Integrate with your calendar to schedule tasks and events. Receive notifications, track the progress of ongoing tasks, and set reminders for upcoming deadlines. Access detailed reports to analyze task completion rates, identify areas for improvement
